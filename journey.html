My Journey

This page shares my journey from my engineering foundation in India to my growth and development in the United Kingdom — a timeline of continuous learning, adaptation, and discovery of where I can make the most impact.

2018 – 2022 · India

Engineering Foundation

I completed my Bachelor’s in Aeronautical Engineering at Sathyabama University, Chennai, India. These four years taught me discipline, technical problem-solving, and teamwork. My final project involved developing a 2U Nano Satellite for atmospheric sensing, which enhanced my understanding of real-world engineering applications and collaboration.

I also participated in technical workshops and seminars, sparking my early curiosity about how systems are designed, optimized, and managed.

2023 – 2024 · United Kingdom

Master’s in Engineering Management

In January 2023, I began my Master’s in Engineering Management at the University of Greenwich. The course integrated engineering and management principles, deepening my understanding of operations, leadership, and planning.

My dissertation focused on “Ethical Considerations on the Use of AI in Healthcare Research.” This project refined my research and analytical skills, and helped me appreciate the importance of ethics and accountability in technology-driven environments.

I graduated in November 2024, marking a proud milestone in my academic and personal journey.

2024 – 2026 · Professional Growth

Career Building, Networking & Certifications

After completing my Master’s, I focused on applying theory to practice through warehouse and logistics roles, gaining hands-on experience in order flow, inventory accuracy, and teamwork.

From March 2025 to January 2026, I attended over 11 professional networking events — learning to communicate confidently, exchange insights, and build meaningful connections with industry professionals.

Supply Chain Foundations

Inventory Management Foundations

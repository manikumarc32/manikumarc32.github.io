My Journey

This page shares my journey so far — from completing my education, moving to the UK, building responsibility through work and study, and gradually developing clarity about my career direction. I am at an entry level, learning step by step, and this page reflects that progress honestly.

2018 – 2022 · India

Engineering Foundation

I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Aeronautical Engineering at Sathyabama University in India. These four years helped me develop discipline, technical thinking, and the ability to work consistently toward long-term goals.

As part of my final-year academic work, I participated in a team-based project involving the development of a 2U Nano Satellite for atmospheric sensing. The project helped me understand system-level thinking, collaboration, and responsibility within a group environment.

During this phase, I also took part in workshops and technical activities, which contributed to my interest in understanding how real systems are designed, built, and managed.

2023 – 2025 · United Kingdom

Master’s in Engineering Management & Personal Growth

In 2023, I moved to the United Kingdom to pursue a Master’s degree in Engineering Management at the University of Greenwich. This transition was a major step in my life, both academically and personally.

The program helped me develop an understanding of operations, planning, coordination, and process-focused thinking. It complemented my engineering background by introducing management perspectives relevant to real-world environments.

For my dissertation, I worked on a research topic titled “Ethical Considerations on the Use of AI in Healthcare Research.” This experience strengthened my research skills and helped me understand the importance of responsibility, ethics, and accountability when applying technology in sensitive domains.

Alongside my studies, I adapted to a new education system, cultural environment, and independent living. I completed my master’s degree on time, which was an important personal achievement for me.

2025 – Present

Professional Growth, Networking & Skill Building

After completing my master’s degree, I focused on gaining practical exposure and understanding how operations function in real working environments. I worked in warehouse and logistics roles, which gave me first-hand experience with order flow, accuracy, teamwork, and responsibility.

During this period, I also started attending networking events in the UK. These events helped me improve my communication, understand industry expectations, and gain confidence in interacting with professionals from different backgrounds.

To strengthen my foundational knowledge, I completed several professional certifications focused on supply chain, inventory management, and ERP systems. These courses helped me understand concepts beyond daily tasks and gave me a structured way to learn.

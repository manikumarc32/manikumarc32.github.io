My Journey

This page explains my academic and professional journey so far — from my engineering education in India to completing my master’s degree in the UK, and my current focus on building a strong foundation for an entry-level career.

2018 – 2022 · India

Bachelor’s in Aeronautical Engineering

I began my higher education in India, where I pursued a Bachelor’s degree in Aeronautical Engineering at Sathyabama University. These four years helped me build a strong technical mindset and discipline required for engineering work.

During my final year, I worked on an academic project involving the development of a 2U Nano Satellite for atmospheric sensing. The project was completed as part of a team and focused on understanding system integration, hardware components, and collaborative problem-solving.

This phase of my life shaped my work ethic, teamwork skills, and ability to handle long-term academic responsibilities.

2023 – 2025 · United Kingdom

Master’s in Engineering Management

After completing my undergraduate degree, I moved to the United Kingdom to pursue a Master’s degree in Engineering Management at the University of Greenwich.

The program introduced me to management principles, operations thinking, and process-focused problem-solving, complementing my engineering background.

For my dissertation, I researched ethical considerations in the use of AI in healthcare research. This work helped me develop critical thinking, structured research skills, and an appreciation for responsibility when applying technology in real-world contexts.

I completed my master’s degree on time while adapting to a new academic system and living environment.

2025 – Present

Transition & Skill Building

After completing my studies, I began focusing on understanding practical operations and logistics environments. I gained hands-on exposure through warehouse and logistics roles in the UK, which helped me understand how systems work on the ground.

Alongside work experience, I started building foundational knowledge in supply chain management, inventory control, SAP ERP systems, and basic data analysis concepts.

I am currently continuing to learn and improve, with the goal of securing an entry-level role where I can grow responsibly, contribute consistently, and develop long-term professional skills.
